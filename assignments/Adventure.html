Adventure

It's monday and you are a monkey at work. A memo comes down through the pipes and it is asking you to answer some questions. They are as follows:

"What color is better?"
"Chicken or Steak?"
"Muscle Car or Import?"

You turn in the memo and the next day you are fired with the comment on your pink slip saying 'He is freaking weird'. You aren't having any of this though so you march into your bosses office. He isn't there but you notice a box of matches. You light one and through it into the trash. You quickly leave the building as everything burns.

You turn in your questionair and the next day you are promoted. You go on a relaxing vacation.
